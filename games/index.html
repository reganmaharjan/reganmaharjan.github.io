Algebrace
Difficulty-Medium
Algebrace is a fast paced and exciting game for kids to practice either arithmetic or algebra problems. Students choose the subject and off they go! Choose the lane with the right answer to gain points and move to the next level as it becomes more and more difficult or you could just crash! The choice is yours.

Sortify
Sortify
Difficulty-Easy
Sortify is an interactive drag and drop game. Students can either choose to sort numbers or animals according to their characteristics with a specific number of points assigned to each category. Students must carefully assign an item to the correct bin category with the most points to get the highest score in the game.

Eat the Pi
Eat the Pi
Difficulty-Medium
Eat the Pi is a fast paced game for kids to learn how to visualize percentage, degree, radians and trigonometric function using a pie. Simply cut the pie when it reaches the displayed value! The pie becomes smaller every time you serve the incorrect amount of pie until it eventually vanishes!

Bina's Adventure
Bina's Adventure
Difficulty-Medium
Bina’s adventure is a fast paced game for kids to learn how to visualize and recognize linear, quadratic, trigonometry and exponential function using a graph. Bina is missing her family. The path to her family follows mathematical functions. Help Bina decode the functions so that she can meet her family.
